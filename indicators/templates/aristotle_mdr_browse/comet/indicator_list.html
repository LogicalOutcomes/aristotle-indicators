    How to find indicators

    This site is a prototype indicator registry, sponsored by LogicalOutcomes and based on the open source Aristotle metadata registry.

    Currently, the registry contains mainly financial literacy indicators sponsored by Prosper Canada. See About for a description of the project and our approach to selecting indicators.

    To explore indicators, select options in the left menu. You can narrow down by topic or type; as the registry grows you will see more search facets.

    Click on the indicator name to see the details, or select up to three indicators to compare them.
